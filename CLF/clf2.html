Teachers Are Being Sent to the Front Lines Unarmed

The most heartbreaking hidden truth: the literacy crisis is not a failure of our children. It is a catastrophic failure of how our society prepares educators. Only 11% of teachers graduate from their preparation programs feeling "completely prepared" to teach early reading.

We ask them to do the most important job in civilization, then give them almost nothing to do it with. CLF steps into that gap, arming dedicated, well-meaning teachers with evidence-based tools they were never given, rescuing them from a systemic betrayal that was never their fault.

We are punishing children for neurological differences they cannot control.

02 ‚Äî The Two-Tier Reality

Literacy Has Quietly Become a Luxury Good

There is an unspoken, two-tiered educational reality in America. When standard classroom instruction fails, families with financial resources quietly spend thousands on private tutors and specialized schools to save their children's futures.

Meanwhile, marginalized communities are trapped in a system without a safety net. For these children, core classroom instruction is the only lifeline. CLF removes the financial barriers, placing the "gold standard" of literacy instruction directly into everyday classrooms and democratizing what was once a privilege reserved for those who could afford it.

Everyone else: left behind
